Beef Wellington

This Beef Wellington is a dish that showcases the perfect harmony between land and sea. This culinary masterpiece features a succulent beef filet mignon wrapped in a layer of mushroom duxelles and buttery puff pastry, tenderly layered with a divine layer of lobster medallions, bringing the ocean's finest flavors to your plate. Served with a side of truffle-infused mashed potatoes and a velvety porcini mushroom sauce, this dish promises to take your taste buds on a delectable journey of textures and flavors.

Stardust Salad

Our Stardust Salad is a cosmic celebration of colors, flavors, and textures, featuring fresh arugula, exotic dragon fruit, ripe avocado, and crunchy pomegranate seeds. Lovingly tossed in our house-made champagne vinaigrette, this salad provides a refreshing and tantalizing experience with each bite. The Stardust Salad is then topped with crumbles of creamy goat cheese, honey-roasted walnuts, and an edible gold leaf for that extra splash of enchantment, making it a perfect appetizer to spark your culinary journey.

Fantasia Flatbread

Prepare to be transported to a realm of epicurean delight with our Fantasia Flatbread, a delightful fusion of worldly flavors served on a canvas of perfectly crisp flatbread. This tantalizingly tasty creation is adorned with a rich layer of mascarpone and fig jam, then generously sprinkled with a colorful medley of tender prosciutto, roasted red peppers, caramelized onions, and dollops of creamy burrata. Finished with a drizzle of aged balsamic glaze and a sprinkle of fresh basil, your taste buds will dance with every delicious bite of our Fantasia Flatbread.
